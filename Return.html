Return & Refund Policy

Last Updated: [Insert Date]
At WZD Store, we strive to ensure you are completely satisfied with your purchase. If you are not happy with a product, we’re here to help with easy returns and refunds.

1. Return Eligibility

You can request a return within 14 days of receiving your order, if:

The product is unused and in its original packaging

Tags, manuals, and accessories are intact

The item is not damaged or broken due to misuse

Proof of purchase (order confirmation or invoice) is available

2. Non-Returnable Items

The following items are not eligible for return or refund:

Used or damaged items

Items without original packaging or accessories

Personalized or custom products

Clearance or sale items marked as “non-returnable”

3. Return Process

To initiate a return:

Email us at support@wzdstore.com within 14 days of delivery.

Mention your order ID and reason for return.

Our team will guide you through the return shipping process.

Once we receive and inspect the item, we’ll notify you of the approval or rejection of your return.

Note: Return shipping cost may be the customer’s responsibility unless the product is defective or wrong.

4. Refund Policy

Approved refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days.

For prepaid orders, the amount will be credited to your original payment method.

For Cash on Delivery (COD) orders, we’ll ask for your bank or UPI details for the refund.

Shipping charges are non-refundable (unless due to seller error).

5. Exchange Policy

We currently do not offer direct product exchanges. You can return the item and place a new order for the desired product.

6. Damaged or Incorrect Products

If your item arrives damaged or you receive the wrong product:

Contact us within 48 hours of delivery

Share clear photos or videos of the damaged/wrong item

We’ll arrange a replacement or full refund at no extra cost to you
